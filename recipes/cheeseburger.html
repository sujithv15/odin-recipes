Cheeseburger recipe

Description: This recipe will teach you how to grill up some bomb ass burgers!

Ingredients List

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Season beef with salt, pepper, and chili powder
  2. Make into burger shapes and cook for 3 min
  3. Cook bacon and put 12 strips on each burger
  4. Put buns over it and eat!
